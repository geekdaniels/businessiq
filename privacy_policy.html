PLEASE NOTE: The BusinessIQ Africa Privacy Policy is effective as of the “Last Updated” date above.

BusinessIQ Africa operates a digital business hub, digital magazine, blog, books and investment platform. The BusinessIQ Africa website allows you to read, connect, collaborate and share documents with other users of BusinessIQ Africa website growing community on the Internet. BusinessIQ Africa’s Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect and use your information. By using or accessing the BusinessIQ Africa website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the BusinessIQ Africa website to stay informed about our information practices.

Information Gathered by BusinessIQ Africa

This Privacy Policy covers our treatment of “personally identifiable” information (meaning it can be used to specifically identify you as a unique person) or “non-personally identifiable” information (meaning it cannot be used to specifically identify you) that we may collect in your use of the BusinessIQ Africa website. We use both types of information, and combinations of both types. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the practices of any other company or individual that we do not own or control.

Visitors: For visitors to our website and registered users, we log certain information, such as your IP address, browser type and usage data, for example, how much time you spent reading, which publications you read, and which pages you accessed.

Registration If you choose to register with us, BusinessIQ Africa may ask you to provide your name, email address and physical address. BusinessIQ Africa may also ask you to create an account ID and password. BusinessIQ Africa may ask you to provide demographic information, such as age, gender, interests or occupation to enhance our ability to provide a more personalized experience for you. We may also, via a third party service, collect credit card information for any paid services or purchases.

Feedback and Chat: We may collect other information when you provide feedback or comments or communicate with us.

Info through other services: You may give us permission to collect your information in other services. For example, you may create an account via a social networking service (“SNS”) such as Facebook or Twitter. When you do this, it allows us to obtain information from those accounts (for example, your friends or contacts) either automatically in accordance with their terms or as a result of your selections in the SNS.

Cookies and Web Beacons: BusinessIQ Africa uses “cookies” – small files containing a string of characters sent to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser – in addition to Flash shared browser objects to administer its website and provide a more tailored and user-friendly service to you. For example, we may use cookies or Flash objects to count the number of unique computers accessing our website. You can set your browser to refuse cookies from the BusinessIQ Africa website if you choose. When you access the BusinessIQ Africa website or open one of our HTML emails, we may automatically record certain information from your system by using different types of tracking technology. This “automatically collected” information may include IP Address, a unique ID, version of software installed, system type, the content and pages that you access on BusinessIQ Africa website, and the dates and times that you visit the BusinessIQ Africa website. You can usually instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.

We assume no liability or responsibility for disclosure of your information due to errors in transmission, unauthorized third-party access, or other causes beyond our control.

To provide and improve the BusinessIQ Africa website. To customize the BusinessIQ Africa website and provide advertisements, content or features that match your interests.
